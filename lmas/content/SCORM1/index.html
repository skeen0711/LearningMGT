SCORM Quiz

Question

What items should be pulled first when fulfilling an order?

Answers

Items with the shortest lengths.

Heaviest items

Items with the highest quantity

Question

Where should longer boards be positioned on the forklift forks?

Answers

At the back next to the mast

On top of the bundle

Flat across the bottom of the bundle

Question

How should you square up the load on the forklift forks?

Answers

Have a helper push the boards straight

Do not attempt to straighten boards on the forks

Use forklift to push lumber against a ground runner to force lumber against the mast

Question

What items should be pulled last and be on top of the bundle?

Answers

The item is next in sequence on the order

Long boards should always be placed on top of the bundle

The shortest items should be pulled last and be on top of the bundle

Question

What document should you have with you while pulling a lumber order?

Answers

A copy of the yard operation procedure

A copy of the order

A link to this video for quick reference

Question

What is the most efficient way to pull lumber orders?

Answers

One person operates the forklift while the other works at the banding station

One person operates the forklift while the other assists by hand-loading lumber onto the forks

One person operates the forklift, then parks it to work at the banding station

Question

Which statement is true about using the forklift forks to pull lumber using the stabbing technique?

Answers

There is little to no risk of damage to the lumber when a beginner operator uses the stabbing technique

Using the forklift forks to stab and pull lumber is an advanced skill

Question

What is the most efficient way to hand-load lumber onto the forklift forks?

Answers

Put the forklift in park while the driver and a helper lift both ends of the lumber to place it on the forks

Put the forklift in park while the driver uses the load as leverage to move lumber onto the forks

Question

What notation should be made on the order copy to indicate an item has been pulled?

Answers

Circle the item number

Cross through the item number

Mark an X over the item quantity

Circle the item quantity

Question

While the forklift is in park and the driver is hand-loading material, what is the primary safety concern?

Answers

Wearing gloves for hand protection

Never allow your body to get in front of or behind the forklift while the engine is running
